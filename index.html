Usability Test

Summary: Testing how usable the design of the Napa Auto Website is with bunch of tasks

Task 1

Find the cheapest antifreeze for RVs

Process: Search for RV, Looking at the price range, finding the cheapest price

Time: 69 seconds

Task 2

Find the closest auto repair location

Process: Looks confused, Goes to location selector, Sees the repair locations

Time: 22 seconds

Task 3

Find instructions on how to replace a drive belt

Process: Goes to “Know How”, Goes to replacing a belt

Time: 24 seconds

Task 4

Find sun shades and place them in the cart

Process: Searches for sun shades, Presses “Add to Cart"

Time: 15 seconds

Task 5

Find the contact information

Process: Scrolls all the way down, Searches for “About NAPA”, Goes to the sidebar

Time: 37 seconds

Overview

In the user's perspective there were a lot of complexity involved in the website and finding certain information could get frustrating.


SUS Score

28 * 2.5 = 70

Above Average

*10 questions were graded between 1 to 5 from least to most agreed
